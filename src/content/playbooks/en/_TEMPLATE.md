<!--
PLAYBOOK WRITING TEMPLATE
=========================
A playbook is a tactical post: "here is exactly what I do." Different rules
than an essay. AI is allowed to draft and structure prose. AI is NOT allowed
to invent the specifics. If a [BRACKET] isn't filled by you, the post does
not ship.

How to use this:
1. Copy this file and rename it (kebab-case, e.g., my-loop.md)
2. Fill the frontmatter (title, date as YYYY-MM-DD, one-line description)
3. Fill every [BRACKET] yourself — these are the parts only you have:
   - The actual prompt(s) you run
   - Real numbers (volume, time, dollars saved)
   - A real example or output
   - The failure mode you've actually hit
4. Ask AI to: tighten transitions, suggest section orders, draft connective
   prose between bracketed specifics, point out generic claims that need a
   number or example
5. Optional but encouraged: include a short footer disclosing AI co-authorship.
   You're writing about AI — transparency is on-brand.

The voice rule: tactical, declarative, numbers-heavy. No hedging.
Specific tools, specific platforms, specific failures.
-->
